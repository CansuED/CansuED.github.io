I am an [Assistant Professor of Information Systems](https://degroote.mcmaster.ca/profiles/ekmekcic/) in the DeGroote School of Business at McMaster University. I direct **D^4 Sustainable Futures Lab**. 

My research examines the use and design of digital technologies to drive managerial, organizational, and community growth, with a particular focus on technology, work, and sustainability in a global context. My academic contributions have been published in venues including Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology, ACM Transactions on Computer-Human Interaction (TOCHI), and the Proceedings of the ACM on Human-Computer Interaction.

I lead *Sustainable Futures Hub* at [The McLean Centre for Collaborative Discovery (MCCD)](https://degroote.mcmaster.ca/about/mclean-centre-for-collaborative-discovery/). I have a Ph.D. in Information Science from the University of Toronto's [Faculty of Information (iSchool)](https://ischool.utoronto.ca/), a Master's Degree in Media and Communication from Galatasaray University, and a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science and International Relations from Bogazici University. In previous lives, I worked as a consultant on technology initiatives in humanitarian and international development contexts and also managed two companies in insurance and information technology.
