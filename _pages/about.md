At McMaster, I direct **D^4 Sustainable Futures Lab**.

I have a Ph.D. in Information Science from the University of Toronto's [Faculty of Information (iSchool)](https://ischool.utoronto.ca/), a Master's Degree in Media and Communication from Galatasaray University, and a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science and International Relations from Bogazici University. In previous lives, I worked as a consultant on technology initiatives in humanitarian and international development contexts and also managed two companies in insurance and information technology.
